About Us (Short Profile of BITM)

Bangladesh Association of Software and Information Services (BASIS) is the national trade body for Software & IT Enabled Service industry of Bangladesh. Established in 1997, the association has been working with a vision of developing vibrant software & IT service industry in the country. Currently BASIS has over 800 members who contribute the majority in the Software & IT service industry in Bangladesh.

BITM is the IT institute of BASIS. To feel the gap of the HR requirement of the IT industry, BITM is working with it.
